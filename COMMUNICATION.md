## Upstream Projects

GigaBot is built upon and inspired by these excellent projects:

### Nanobot by HKUDS

The original lightweight AI assistant framework that GigaBot builds upon.

- **Repository**: [github.com/HKUDS/nanobot](https://github.com/HKUDS/nanobot)
- **Community**: HKUDS provides WeChat and Feishu discussion groups for the original Nanobot project

<img src="https://github.com/HKUDS/.github/blob/main/profile/QR.png" alt="HKUDS WeChat QR Code" width="300"/>

### OpenClaw

Major architectural inspiration for GigaBot's enterprise features.

- **Repository**: [github.com/openclaw/openclaw](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw)
- **Website**: [openclaw.ai](https://openclaw.ai)
- **Discord**: [discord.gg/clawd](https://discord.gg/clawd)
